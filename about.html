WashU Club Running

Do you love to run but want a buddy? Are you training for a half marathon and want some practice? Do you want to have a seriously good time and stay in shape all at once? Then WashU Club Running is for you! We meet for a run Sunday-Thursday at 5:30pm at the clock tower on the South 40. We also run subsidized races throughout the semester. We also dedicate a large amount of time to organizing the Take Steps for Kids 5K and 1M. So come have an amazing time with us!
