WashU Club Running

We are WashU's Club Running Team. We are co-ed and have runners of all commitments and abilities, so come join us Sunday-Thursday at 5:30pm at the Clock Tower. We compete both within the St. Louis area and against other club teams across the nation. We are a part of Washington University's Sports Club Board and the National Intercollegiate Running Club Association. We run heavily subsidized races numerous times a semester and have a great time no matter how we do.
