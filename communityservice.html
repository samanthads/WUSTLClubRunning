Take Steps For Kids 5k and 1M

Previous Next
Our biggest event is the Take Steps For Kids 5K and 1 Mile race, which we host each spring at WashU. We love to give back to the St. Louis community, and this is our chance. The 100% of the revenue from the race benefits Big Brothers Big Sisters of Eastern Missouri. We spend the day running and participating in match activities with Bigs and Littles, and just plain having a great time! Join us for an afternoon of fun benefitting a great cause! Visit TakeStepsForKids.org for more information.
